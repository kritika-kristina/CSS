Polynomials Intro

Basic Info

A polynomial is an expression of more than two algebraic terms, especially the sum of several terms that contain different powers of the same variable(s).

There are 3 types of Polynomials

A Monomial is a polynomial with one term. A Binomial is a polynomial with two terms. A Trinomial is a polynomial with three terms.

Degree of a Polynomial

The Degree of a Polynomial is the degree that the variable is being raised to.
